About us


GDG Leiria is part of the 700+ GDG Communities all over the world and the 5th to appear in Portugal.

 

This is a community of technology enthusiasts and developers, whose aim is to promote events (conferences and workshops) related to the world of development, innovation and technology and also promote the share of knowledge between all members.

 

We are always open to new ideas for events or activities from everyone, so that we can improve and try to please every taste.

 

Feel free to contact us and suggest your ideas!
